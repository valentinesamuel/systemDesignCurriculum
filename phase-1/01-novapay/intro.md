# NovaPay — Company Introduction

NovaPay is a Series B fintech startup building a Stripe-like payment infrastructure
platform for emerging markets — think Southeast Asia and West Africa, where card
rails are unreliable, mobile money is king, and regulatory complexity is brutal.
The engineering team is ~40 people strong, moving fast, and sitting on a pile of
venture capital with a mandate to 10x transaction volume in 18 months.

You join as a **Mid-Level Backend Engineer** on the Payments Core team. Your hiring
manager, **Dani Osei**, is a sharp Senior Engineer who has built payment systems at
two prior startups and has exactly zero patience for hand-wavy architecture. The CTO,
**Priya Sharma**, runs a weekly "architecture hour" where teams present their designs
for peer review — sometimes supportively, sometimes with surgical brutality.

Your first day starts with a Slack message from Dani: *"Welcome. Don't unpack yet.
We have a problem."*
