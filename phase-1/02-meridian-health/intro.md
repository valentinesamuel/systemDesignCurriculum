# MeridianHealth — Company Introduction

MeridianHealth is a Series B health-tech company building a unified EHR (Electronic
Health Records) integration platform for mid-sized hospital networks in the United States.
Think of them as the "Plaid for healthcare" — they connect fragmented hospital systems,
legacy HL7 feeds, and modern FHIR APIs into a single normalized data platform that
enables clinical analytics, care coordination, and patient engagement apps.

The regulatory environment is unforgiving: HIPAA governs every piece of data, every
API call, every database query. A breach doesn't just mean customer churn — it means
federal fines, potential criminal liability, and the end of the company.

You join as a **Senior Backend Engineer** on the Platform team. Your manager, **Ravi
Chandran**, is experienced and careful — maybe too careful. The previous tech lead left
abruptly after a disagreement about "cutting corners on audit infrastructure." Nobody
will say more than that.

Your first day, Ravi walks you through the codebase. "We're a good team," he says,
carefully. "We just need someone who takes compliance seriously and can also ship."
