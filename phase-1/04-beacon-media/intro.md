# Beacon Media — Company Introduction

Beacon Media is a streaming platform serving 28 million monthly active users across
Southeast Asia and Sub-Saharan Africa. They stream video-on-demand (movies, TV series,
original content), live sports, and short-form video. Think of them as a regional
streaming service that competes with Netflix and YouTube in markets where mobile data
is expensive and network conditions are unpredictable.

The engineering team is ~80 people and has been doubling headcount every 12 months.
The infrastructure is at an inflection point: the CDN strategy was designed for
5 million users and is now serving 28 million. Content delivery costs are the
second-largest line item in the company budget, and the Head of Engineering, **Fatima Ould**,
has a mandate from the board to "fix the CDN situation before it eats the company."

You join as a **Senior Backend Engineer** on the Infrastructure team. You passed the
Helios Systems checkpoint and took this offer instead — Fatima sold you on the scale
of the problems. "We're spending $2M a month on CDN egress," she said. "That number
needs to go down."

Your first day, she shows you the Grafana dashboard. The CDN hit rate is 54%.
"It should be 92%," she says. "Find out why it isn't."
