## The Company

BuildRight manages 40,000 active construction projects across 80 countries. General contractors, subcontractors, architects, and building inspectors all live inside it — RFIs, submittals, permit workflows, punch lists, material orders, safety logs. At any given moment, a BuildRight outage means someone somewhere can't file a permit, can't approve a change order, can't sign off on a steel delivery.

CEO Thomas Adeyemi spent 15 years as a structural engineer before pivoting to software. He still talks about load-bearing walls when he means database schemas. He is not joking. He cares about durability, tolerances, and what happens when something fails under load. He expects the same from his systems.

The previous Staff Architect left eight months ago to "spend more time with family." Nobody says what actually happened.
