# OrbitCore — Company Introduction

## Industry
Space Tech / Satellite Operations Platform

## Company Profile

OrbitCore is a Series B (soon to be Series C) SaaS company that operates the ground-side intelligence layer for commercial satellite constellations. Founded in 2018 by three former aerospace engineers who were tired of watching billion-dollar satellites underperform because the ground software was terrible, OrbitCore sells a platform: real-time telemetry ingestion, orbital health monitoring, anomaly detection, and ground-station orchestration. Their customers include constellation operators, defense contractors (unclassified tier only), and a growing cluster of climate-monitoring nonprofits.

As of your start date, OrbitCore operates 12 ground stations across 6 countries (US, UK, Germany, Kenya, Singapore, Australia), maintains telemetry pipelines for 47 active satellites across 4 customer constellations, and processes roughly 1.8–2.1 million telemetry events per second during peak orbital windows. The engineering team is 34 people. You are the third Staff Engineer.

## Your Role

You join as Staff Engineer, Distributed Systems — reporting to Ingrid Solberg, VP of Engineering. Your mandate is loosely defined in the offer letter as "own the reliability and scalability of the ingestion and ground-station coordination layer." In practice, Ingrid will clarify this on day one: you are here because the system is about to be asked to handle 200 satellites and nobody is confident it can handle 47.
